Noxus History

Before Noxus

Noxus was first formed by mordakaiser, a dead warlord brought back by sorcerors. He created the immortal bastion, the capital of Noxus. Mordakaiser was then defeated by the surrounding tribes called the Noxei.

Start of The Expansion

After they defeated Mordakaiser, they created the empire of Noxus as the Immortal bastion its center capital. After creating Noxus, the people expanded untill the other countries used powerful crystals that created the world. Because of this the world was destroying itself. So the Noxians retreated to the Immortal Bastion to get out of the path of destruction.

The Founding of Noxus

After the wielders of the crystals detroyed each other, they came out more united than ever as one unit. They continued theyre conquest and took over alot of land. It was easy untill they met damacia. Demacia was able to push back noxus almost to its capital after killing one of their major warlords, Sion, but he was able to slay the king of Damacia.

Ionian Invasion

At the same time The emperor of Noxus was old and seeking eternal life, so he sent an invasion force to Ionia. Ionia was made of self governing tribes with almost no army. Therefore when Noxus invaded Ionia, in search of magical artifacts to prolong the emperor's life, Ionia stood no chance. Noxus was able to easily reach the holy place of Ionia and ship all its artifacts back to Noxus. Thats when Ionia started to rebel using its martial arts and dance forms and turning them into weapons. They were able to push back Noxus. Thats why Swain, a general in Noxus, was almost killed.

The rebellion and the creation of the Trifarix

As he was about to die a crow came and told him of darkness in Noxus. He found out that the emperor of Noxus was being controlled by LeBlanc, who Swain thought was dead. So he returned to the capital and got the power of a demon. Using his political influence, he was able to get the support of many nobles, one being katarina's dad. With the support of the nobles, Swain was able to kill the emperor and take over Noxus. With control of Noxus, he created the Trifarix. A group of three poeple to rule different parts of noxus and keep eachother in check. The three poeple being Swain, Darius, and LeBlanc. Which sums up the hisotry of noxus to where you can understand what the trifarix is.
