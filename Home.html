What is League of Legends Lore?

League of Legends lore is the backstory of the game League of legends. It explains where each champion is from, what culture they are from and this shapes who they are. It is the world of League of Legends past the video game, made by the creators of League of Legends.

Why is it important?

League of Legends lore is important to avid players of league of legends because it brings in characterization to characters and helps the players imagine a personality related to the land and culture that the specific champion comes from.

The World of League of Legends

The world of league of legends is seperated into 13 regions, each with they're own laws, religions, cultures, traditions, and technologies. The 13 regions being Bandle City, BilgeWater, Demacia, Ionia, Ixtal, Noxus, Piltover, Shadow Isles, Shurima, Targon, The Freljord, The Void, and finally Zaun.
